Lumisound Privacy Policy: Lumisound does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Microphone access is used solely for real-time audio analysis on your device. Audio is never recorded to any server or shared with any third party. Photos library access is used solely to save spectrogram images at your request. No images are transmitted or stored outside your device. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used. Last updated: March 2026
